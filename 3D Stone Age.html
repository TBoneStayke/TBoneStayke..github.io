Assets I Modeled for my Individual AR Game

GIMM 370

Description

For my individual Immersive 3D game named "Mini-Game Mayhem" I had to model personal assets for my game. After modeling these assets in blender I then exported them as .fbx files and threw them into substance painter where I added texture and volume to the models. I then exported the different texture maps to use for assets inside Unity. These are a couple examples of models that I made and textured for my game.

Skills Used

  • Basic 3D Modeling Skills
  • Blender Tools and Functionality
  • Photoshop for Alpha Masking
  • Substane Painter for Texturing
