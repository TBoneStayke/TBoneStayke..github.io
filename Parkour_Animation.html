Parkour Animation

GIMM 100











Description:

For this project I combined the skills I have with parkour in real life, and I combined them with my skills I learned for animation and rhotoscopy in GIMM 100. I decided to take 4 of my favorite parkour tricks and explain them through a simple animation. I drew all of these tricks frame per frame creating the rhotoscopy you see in these gifs. This was a fun project and I really enjoyed combining two of my hobbies into a GIMM project for the semester.

Skills Used

  • Animation rhotoscopy and animating text
  • Different skills involved with all of the Adobe Creative Cloud apps
